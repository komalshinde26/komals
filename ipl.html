Rohit Sharma has many achievements in the Indian Premier League (IPL), including:
Five IPL titles: As captain of the Mumbai Indians, Sharma led the team to five IPL titles in 2013, 2015, 2017, 2019, and 2020. He shares this record with MS Dhoni for the most successful captain in IPL history.
First IPL century: Sharma scored his first and only IPL century in 2011.
Top-scorer for Mumbai Indians: Sharma is the top-scorer for the Mumbai Indians.
One of only four batters with more than 6,000 runs: Sharma is one of only four batters with more than 6,000 runs in the IPL.
IPL debut: Sharma joined the IPL in 2008 with the Deccan Chargers, who won the second edition of the IPL in 2009.
Hat-trick: Sharma took a hat-trick against the Mumbai Indians in 2009 with his part-time off-spin.
Sharma is also known for his calm demeanor and tactical acumen on the field.
